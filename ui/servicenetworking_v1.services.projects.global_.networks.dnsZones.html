Service Networking API . services . projects . global_ . networks . dnsZones

* Service producers can use this method to retrieve a list of available DNS zones in the shared producer host project and the matching peering zones in the consumer project. *

name: string, Required. The network that the consumer is using to connect with services. Must be in the form of services/{service}/projects/{project}/global/networks/{network}/dnsZones/{zoneName} Where {service} is the peering service that is managing connectivity for the service producer's organization. For Google services that support this {project} is the project number, as in '12345' {network} is the network name. {zoneName} is the DNS zone name (required)
